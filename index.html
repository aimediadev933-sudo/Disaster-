Strengthening Regional Disaster Preparedness

LandSAGE represents a groundbreaking initiative that combines cutting-edge visualization technology with collaborative capacity-building across Southeast Asia. Our project deployed CyberCANOE systems running SAGE2 to create an unprecedented platform for disaster preparedness and response.
